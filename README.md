# Minimalist

Minimalist is a simple and lightweight to-do app that supports tasks, projects, and note-taking capabilities. The app is designed to provide an intuitive and clutter-free experience for managing your tasks and staying organized.

## Features

- Manage tasks within projects for better organization.
- Take notes and keep important information at your fingertips.
- Mark tasks as done and keep track of your progress.
- Utilize local storage to persist your data across sessions.
